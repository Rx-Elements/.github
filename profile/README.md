## What is iZotope RX?

**iZotope RX** is the industry-standard audio repair and restoration software used by professionals worldwide for cleaning and enhancing audio. It is an indispensable tool for audio engineers, podcasters, filmmakers, and musicians who need to salvage imperfect recordings or achieve broadcast-quality sound. RX provides a powerful suite of modules designed to tackle any audio problem, from simple noise reduction to complex spectral repair.

With iZotope RX, users can effortlessly remove unwanted noises like background hiss, microphone rumble, plosives, clicks, and hums. Its groundbreaking **Spectral Editing** interface allows you to see and manipulate audio in a detailed spectrogram, enabling surgical precision when removing individual sounds without affecting the surrounding material. Whether you're restoring old archives, cleaning up dialogue for film, or perfecting a music track, RX offers an intuitive yet powerful workflow.

The software is renowned for its AI-assisted features, such as **Dialogue Isolate** and **Music Rebalance**, which intelligently separate elements within a mix. This makes it possible to reduce noise behind speech or adjust the levels of vocals, bass, drums, and other instruments after recording. iZotope RX is the go-to solution for achieving pristine, professional audio quality in any project.
